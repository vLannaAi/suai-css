Main content

The simplest page shell: a header at the top, a flexible main area that grows to fill the viewport, and a footer that sits at the bottom of the screen.

Because main takes the remaining height, the footer never floats up into the middle of the page on short content — a classic "sticky footer" without any extra wrappers.

When to use

  • Content pages, articles, documentation
  • Anything that needs a header and a footer but no sidebar
