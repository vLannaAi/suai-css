Main content with a related sidebar

The primary content flows in main, while a complementary aside holds related material. On mobile the aside stacks below the content; on wide screens (≥1024px) it becomes a right-hand sidebar.

The header spans the full width above both columns and the footer spans below them, so the two-column band sits neatly in the middle.

Body

Use this pattern for blog posts, documentation with a table of contents, or product pages with a specs panel.

Resize the window past 1024px to watch the aside move from below the content to the side.
